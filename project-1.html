Project Overview:

This Microsite development project requires us to select a client, curate, and write content, define a specific group of users with a specific need, structure the content, and complete a full interactive mockup of the microsite.

Our group create a site based on the information of BC SPCA(British Columbia Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals), we aim to address the need of people that are looking for dogs to adopt. Our microsite consists of images, detailed information about the available dogs for adoption, and contact forms for further action. Our primary purpose was to create a welcoming atmosphere through design and provide direct, helpful data for our aimed user group through the content structure.
